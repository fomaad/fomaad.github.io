Our research group focus on advancing the safety and reliability of next-generation automotive systems, specifically, autonomous driving systems. Due to the inherent complexity of these systems, rigorous testing and verification are crucial to ensure their safe and reliable operation under various real-world conditions. We specialize in applying formal methods and developing verification tools to address these challenges. By bridging the gap between theoretical approaches and real-world applications, we aim to make autonomous driving safer and more dependable, contributing to the realization of a reliable mobility society.
